INTRODUCTION

Designing spaces that look good and work well

Form describes how a room looks, while function describes how well it supports everyday life. Thoughtful interior design brings these two ideas together through color, lighting, furniture, materials, texture, and layout. This website explores how those choices can transform the spaces we call home.

DESIGN PRINCIPLES

What makes a space work?

01

Balance

Distribute visual weight so the room feels stable and intentional.

02

Contrast

Combine differences in color, shape, or texture to add interest.

03

Lighting

Layer natural, ambient, task, and accent lighting for different needs.

04

Flow

Arrange furniture so people can move easily through the room.

05

Color

Use a consistent palette to create mood and connect the space.

06

Scale

Choose furniture and décor that fit the size and proportions of the room.

07

Texture

Mix soft, smooth, woven, and natural surfaces to create depth.

08

Function

Make every design choice support the purpose of the room.
